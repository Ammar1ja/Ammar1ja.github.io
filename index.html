Hello, I'm Ammar Jaber, a passionate computer science student at Al-Isra University in Jordan. Graduating in 2024, I'm filled with anticipation and enthusiasm for the future. My academic journey has ignited a deep love for programming and a drive to stay ahead of the curve in this ever-evolving field. I thrive on the opportunity to learn new concepts and technologies, adapting quickly to the latest advancements. One of my proudest achievements is being a part of the esteemed One Million Jordanian Coders initiative, where I earned a nano-degree certificate. This immersive experience not only expanded my coding prowess but also connected me with a network of like-minded individuals devoted to advancing the field. Additionally, I had the privilege of engaging in meaningful discussions during a dialogue session organized by the Crown Prince Foundation, alongside fellow graduates on September 1, 2019. Beyond my studies, I have pushed myself by participating in the Jordanian Collegiate Programming Contest (JCPC). The competition, held at Irbid Private University on October 10, 2022, allowed me to further sharpen my problem-solving skills. As I journey towards graduation, I eagerly seek new ventures to apply my skills and knowledge, contributing to the exciting realm of computer science. Thank you for taking the time to get to know me.
